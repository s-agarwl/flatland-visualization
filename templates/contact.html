PomVis: Visualization for Analysis of Pommerman Games
(Please fill the feedback form to help us in research and suggest further improvements.)


This is a research prototype to enable visual analysis of games played in Pommerman. The visualization is being developed with the aim of helping the developers who train AI agents to play the game. The visualization would also be helpful for evaluators of the competition where AI agents are usually compared across several games. We invite Pommerman community members to use the prototype and explore the different behaviors of their trained agents. Please feel free to contact us and share your experience of using the prototype. Also, please report any bugs you find in the tool.

We urge the users of this tool to please fill out this feedback form. It will help us in the research and make further improvements in the tool.
